Section 2: Steal Analysis (separate from batting xRV)

Steal defense per team = avg over all pitcher-catcher pairs on that team of (pitcher AWR + catcher EYE) / 2.

Steal EV = P(safe) * RE_gain + (1-P(safe)) * RE_loss where RE values are from the 12 run expectancy table.

Break-even P(safe) for stealing 2nd = 68.7%. Stealing 3rd is excluded (STEAL_TABLE max success rate 51.7% < ~71% break-even).
